About Eighth Note Games

Eighth Note Games is a one-man independent game studio based in Salt Lake City, Utah. I am currently working on an interactive fiction called "Branches of the Past" (check it out on the projects page!). Some smaller experimental projects can be found on my itch.io page.

I'm Jeremy Dormitzer. I'm an undergraduate computer science major at Westminster College in Salt Lake City. I do game development and web development. Find me on LinkedIn or contact me by email.
