Branches of the Pastclose

Branches of the Past is an interactive fiction exploring Adriata, an ancient city where the streets conceal tangled secrets and the walls remember what people have forgotten. It is inspired by Italo Calvino's Invisible Cities.

The story is told in a series of vignettes. The vignettes can be played in any order, and the choices made in one vignette affect the narrative of the others. It is due for release in late April. For excerpts and more information, check out the blog!
